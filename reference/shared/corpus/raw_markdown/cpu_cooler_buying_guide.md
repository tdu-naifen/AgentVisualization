# How to Choose a CPU Cooler: Buying Guide

Picking the right CPU cooler keeps your CPU temperature in check. This guide
helps you service and upgrade your CPU cooler.

## CPU Cooler Types
- Air CPU coolers: cheap, reliable, easy to service.
- Liquid CPU coolers: lower CPU temperature, quieter, pricier.

## Matching the CPU Socket
Always match the CPU cooler to your CPU socket. A mismatched CPU cooler will not
seat on the CPU. Most repair services stock CPU cooler brackets for every CPU.

## Servicing Your CPU Cooler
Re-apply thermal paste when you service the CPU cooler. A good service interval
keeps CPU temperature low and extends CPU cooler service life. Our service team
recommends cleaning the CPU cooler fins twice a year.
